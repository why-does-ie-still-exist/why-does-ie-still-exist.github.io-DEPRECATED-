SkillsUSA is one of the most successful clubs at CK High School in many aspects. In 95 different competitions students have the chance to test a wide range of skills against other competitors; from Culinary Arts to Computer Animation. SkillsUSA is the premiere leading organization for furthering students’ knowledge and skills in technical education. SkillsUSA has also been recognized by the US Government as the official and only organization to represent the USA in front of the world at the World Skills Competition.

SkillsUSA offers a competitive environment in which to test your abilities.
As a member, you receive leadership and occupational skills that you’ll use
far after you graduate and gain a truly amazing experience.

Some other benefits include:

  • Colleges and businesses highly regard SkillsUSA making it look great on applications and resumes.
  • Do you like to travel? If you join SkillsUSA, you’ll have the opportunity to travel across the state and potentially the nation.
  • Enjoy fun activities during the various conferences as a time to take a break from competing and to meet new people.
